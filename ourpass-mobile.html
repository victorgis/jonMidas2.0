Introduction

The OurPass Mobile app had a simple goal (well not so simple when you really think about it), help shoppers checkout!!!. Simple right? I guess so, unfortunately though being simple and being done happen to be two extremes.

Currently, Nigeria is one of the biggest markets when it comes to fintech products in Africa. A quick look at most fintech products in Nigeria, and you’ll be sure to find the classic send and receive money features, upon which other features are then built upon.

In keeping up with this tradition, the goal of the OurPass mobile app was to help online shoppers buy and pay for products online with as much ease as possible, by giving them access to platforms that had integrated the OurPass Checkout button on the app. This coupled with the classic send and receive money feature, would help give the app more use cases.

This meant that emphasis would be placed on the checkout experience, while the send and receive money feature will serve as an addendum.

Changes

I joined the team as part of efforts to help improve the overall experience of its available products offerings in the market. We however at some point had to rethink the entire experience of the mobile app, especially as regards the checkout experience. After weeks of gathering user feedback, our fears became justified: Most users merely saw the mobile app as another fintech app for sending, receiving money, and paying bills, and this was totally not what we had initially intended. At this point, our mission became redefined: (Your Mission Should You Choose to Accept is to) Redesign the OurPass Mobile App.

And so it Began

We began by creating a list of items on the existing app that were supposed to serve as addendums, we separated these from those items that were an integral part of the checkout process, this helped us decide what we would prioritize or de-prioritize.

One of the goals of the redesign was simplicity. The existing app had some redundant elements and sometimes involved more steps than necessary to carry out simple tasks, we aimed to change this also.

Hence, the goal of the redesign became clear:

1. Design the OurPass Mobile app to help online shoppers Checkout in one click!!!
2. Reduce users efforts, by reducing number of clicks needed for each action
3. Design a more pleasurable interface.

UI Designs

The entire goal of the redesign was to create an experience that’d help users checkout on the OurPass mobile app in one click, hence our redesign had to go through vigorous checks at various times; these checks were done mostly through remote unmoderated user tests, and this helped us gain massive insight as regards users perception of our designs.
