Introduction

MoreNiche is an Affiliate Network based in Nottingham (UK) which specialise in the health and beauty vertical. With a passion for generating client ROI MoreNiches operate a very different model to most traditional affiliate networks. I was tasked with the responsibility of creating an experience that’d help brands, partners and affiliates alike get the most of the Moreniche business model through an intuitive and simple interface that takes out all the work from users.

Conclusion

I joined the team as part of efforts to help improve the overall experience of its available products offerings in the market. We however at some point had to rethink the entire experience of the mobile app, especially as regards the checkout experience. After weeks of gathering user feedback, our fears became justified: Most users merely saw the mobile app as another fintech app for sending, receiving money, and paying bills, and this was totally not what we had initially intended.
